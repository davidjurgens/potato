Describe your annotation task in detail. What should be labeled? What are the categories? What makes something belong to one category vs another?

What happens next?

  1. Prompt Generation: An LLM will generate an annotation prompt based on your description
  2. Prompt Review: You can review and edit the generated prompt
  3. Edge Cases: The LLM will generate difficult examples for you to label
  4. Parallel Annotation: You and the LLM will annotate in parallel
  5. Disagreement Resolution: You'll resolve any disagreements
  6. Autonomous Labeling: Once agreement is high, the LLM completes the dataset
  7. Validation: You'll validate a sample of LLM-only labels
