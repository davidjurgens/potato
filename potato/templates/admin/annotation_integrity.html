Annotation integrity

Flags annotators who likely echoed an LLM or answered with little effort — without ground truth. Correlated Agreement (CA) is each annotator's same-item minus cross-item agreement with peers (low ⇒ random/low effort). When reference LLM labels are available, LLM alignment with low residual (independent agreement on items where they diverge from the LLM) is the covert-LLM tell. Treat as triage signals to review, not proof.
