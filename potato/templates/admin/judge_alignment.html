Auto-calibrate

Close the loop automatically: the instances where a human corrected the judge (human label ≠ judge label) are its most informative examples. POST to /admin/api/judge-alignment/autocalibrate to re-run the judge with those corrections injected as few-shot examples (leakage-guarded — an instance never sees its own correction), creating a new prompt version whose mean κ is compared against the baseline. Optional body: {"max_corrections": 5, "max_per_schema": N}. The new version appears in Prompt versions above.
